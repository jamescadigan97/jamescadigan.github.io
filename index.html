I have attached a project in order to demonstrate some of the skills I have acquired during my time in the bootcamp. My project comprises of various dynamic visualizations about renewable energy. It contains a heatmap showing the locations of solar, wind and hydro-electric power-plants. It also has figures that display the generation capacities of wind, solar hydroelectric energy in different states. The last page shows a sunburst map distribution of energy generation in the United States and along with web-scraped articles on current advances in renewable energy. This project ultilizes Leaflet for the heatmap, marker map and cloropeth maps and Plotly JavaScript for the sunburst map. The website is powered by Flask and launched on Amazon Web Serivces. The webscraping function employs chromedriver, Beautiful Soup to read the data and MongoDB to store it.
